    About me

    Chorfi Khaoula

    Pharmacist specialized in Hydrology and Bromatology (study of water and food). Certified specialist in therapeutic nutrition from CFNA (Center for Applied Nutritherapy and Phytotherapy Training). Certified Trainer in Therapeutic Nutrition by the International Labor Organization I.L.O

    This platform specializes in the field of obesity and overweight. We don't focus on implementing diets, but on changing dietary and daily habits, such as sleep, exercise, and screen exposure (TV, smartphone).
